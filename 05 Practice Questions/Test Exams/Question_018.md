Quick recap of why the others are eliminated:

- **Apache Kafka ingestion** → That's the **Kafka Connector** or **Snowpipe Streaming**, not Openflow
- **Export to external messaging systems** → Describes a narrow outbound-only tool; Openflow is bidirectional and broader
- **Cross-region table replication** → That's **Snowflake Database Replication**, a separate feature entirely

Openflow = Snowflake's answer to Fivetran/Airbyte, built natively into the platform. Any source, any destination, batch or streaming.
