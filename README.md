## <img src="https://api.iconify.design/ph/book-open-light.svg?color=%231F2937&width=22&height=22" width="22" /> Before You Start

- **Don't try to memorize everything.** Understand the *why* behind each concept.
- **One topic per day is enough.** Consistency beats cramming.
- **SQL is your weapon.** Practice writing queries, not just reading them.
- **The exam is scenario-based.** It will describe a business situation and ask what you'd do.

## <img src="https://api.iconify.design/ph/brain-light.svg?color=%231F2937&width=22&height=22" width="22" /> The Mindset

> *"The exam rewards people who understand how Snowflake thinks — not people who memorized syntax."*

Every time you learn a concept, ask yourself:

1. What problem does this solve?
2. What would break if I did this wrong?
3. What scenario on the exam might test this?

You've got 60 days. That's more than enough.
